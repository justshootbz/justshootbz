About Us

History

Just Shoot Productions was founded in 2016 by Dwayne Cus and Rudolph Parham with a mission to supply the great country of Belize with studio-quality graphic media at affordable prices.

Mission

We strive to put our client's best foot forward through the highest quality work to showcase their business, products, and most special moments.

Vision

We seek to change the mentality of shooting guns to shooting cameras. Through our work we hope to improve not only our client's portfolio, but to improve our community as well.
